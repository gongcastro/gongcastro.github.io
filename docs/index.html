I am a PhD student in Biomedicine at the Center for Brain and Cognition, at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (Barcelona, Spain). I study how bilingual toddlers develop their vocabulary, using both experimental and observational techniques. I am particularly interested in making my research as reproducible as possible. I use R to collect and analyse my data, and to generate reports. I love sharing my work and, especially my learning progress, in my blog. You will mostly find Bayesian stuff, tutorials on statistics, and some experiences as an R and Julia user.
