About

I am a PhD student at the Center for Brain and Cognition, at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (Barcelona, Spain). I study how bilingual toddlers develop their vocabulary, using both experimental and observational techniques. Learning new things about programming languages brings me joy. I mainly use Twitter for sharing research outputs, programming tips or data visualisations. Don’t hesitate to get in touch on Twitter (@gongcastro) or at gonzalo.garciadecastro@upf.edu!
